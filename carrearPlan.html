  • Learn Additional Languages: I aim to improve my skills in additional programming languages like TypeScript, Swift (for iOS development), or PHP to broaden my technical expertise.

  • Secure an Internship: I will apply for internships at companies like MAYTECH or similar firms that focus on web development. This will provide me with practical experience and professional connections.

    Professional Development
